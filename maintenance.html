Maintenance Guide

Keep your generator running smoothly with proper care

Maintenance Tips

  • Regular Oil Changes: Check and change the oil regularly as per manufacturer recommendations. Clean oil keeps the engine running efficiently and extends its lifespan.
  • Air Filter Maintenance: Inspect air filters and clean or replace them to maintain proper airflow. A clogged filter reduces efficiency and can damage the engine.
  • Battery Testing: Test the battery and charging system regularly if your generator has one. Clean corroded terminals and ensure proper connections.
  • Periodic Operation: Run the generator periodically, even when not in use, to keep it in good working order. This prevents fuel from staling and keeps internal components lubricated.
  • Proper Storage: Store the generator in a dry, covered area to prevent rust and damage. Use fuel stabilizer if storing for extended periods.
  • Spark Plug Inspection: Check spark plugs regularly and replace if worn or fouled. Clean plugs ensure proper ignition and smooth operation.
  • Fuel System Care: Keep the fuel system clean and free of contaminants. Use fresh, high-quality fuel and replace fuel filters as recommended.
